# UmaKuma Extended Brand Prompt

Use this after [BRAND_CORE.md](BRAND_CORE.md) has produced approved mascot designs.

## Locked Identity

Carry forward the approved UmaKuma mascot style exactly.

- Horse is Uma.
- Bear is Kuma.
- In paired scenes, horse is always left and bear is always right.
- Horse keeps one bent/limp ear and a small hair tuft.
- Bear keeps one opposite-color accent eye and a small hair tuft.
- Use the same thick-line sticker/badge style, colors, proportions, and face details from the approved core assets.

Palette:
- Primary blue: #2D7CFF
- Coral: #FF5D73
- Mint: #23C6A8
- Soft sky: #EEF4FF
- Text navy: #16223A
